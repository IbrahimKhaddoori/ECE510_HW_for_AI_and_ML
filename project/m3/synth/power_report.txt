# Power estimation report

## Attempt

Power estimation was attempted using OpenLane 2's STA_REPORT_POWER option on Google Colab. The full OpenLane flow failed at the Verilator lint stage (Colab's Verilator 4.x lacks the -Wno-EOFNEWLINE flag required by OpenLane 2) and at the Yosys JSON header stage (Colab's Yosys 0.9 lacks the -y flag used by OpenLane 2's pyosys wrapper). Synthesis was completed using standalone Yosys, but Yosys 0.9 does not include power estimation.

## Failure mode

OpenLane 2 requires Yosys ≥ 0.23 and Verilator ≥ 5.0 for the full flow including power analysis. Google Colab provides Yosys 0.9 and Verilator 4.x via apt, which are too old. The Nix-based OpenLane installation was not attempted due to Colab storage constraints.

## Rough estimate from synthesis data

Based on the 95,027 synthesized cells in sky130_fd_sc_hd at 83 MHz:
- 11,902 flip-flops (dominant static power contributor)
- 56,428 MUX cells (dominant dynamic power due to high switching during weight reads)
- 8 MAC lanes switching every cycle during S_MAC state

Estimated dynamic power: ~5-15 mW at 83 MHz (based on sky130 typical cell power of ~0.5-1.5 µW/cell/MHz for active cells, with ~30% average activity factor).

Estimated leakage power: ~50-200 µW (sky130 130nm process, room temperature).
